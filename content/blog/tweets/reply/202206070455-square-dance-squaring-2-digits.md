I use this trick to square any 2 digit number in my head: square last digit, multiple both digits and double, square first digit, manage carries. 

For example,

6*6=36 (keep 6, carry 3)
2*6*2=24 + carry 3=27 (keep 7, carry 2)
2*2=4+ carry 2=6

Ans: 676
